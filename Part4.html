About PSA

PSA aims at removing, to the barest minimum, Mathematics Phobia in all and sundry. This is to be done by bringing everyone to the realization that Mathematics is the easiest subject so far.

About PSD

PSD is the Director of PSA, whose sole interest lies in eradicating the fear of Mathematics that has eaten deep into the intestines and livers of most Nigerian students.
